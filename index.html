About

Curious by nature.
Grounded by values.

I believe that most things worth understanding take time, honesty, and a willingness to be wrong. I'm drawn to questions that resist easy answers — about how things work, why people do what they do, and what it means to live deliberately.

This site is a place for my work, my writing, and my ongoing attempt to learn in public. Some of what I share here will be half-formed. That's the point. Truth rarely arrives finished.

When I'm not building things or reading, I'm probably asking questions I don't have answers to yet — and I wouldn't have it any other way.
